THE STORY

Like all lovely fantistic lovers, Sven and Rallys met in a great place on earth. In the capital of Portugal, which is Lisbon in case you didn't know.

Sven had only just arrived in the sunny capital until he stumbled on a nice blurry picture of Rallys on a highly esteemed dating app called Grinder and seemed it wise to meet up on a beach with this complete stranger.

Shockingly he actually turned out to be an amazing person, guess you can never know from a blurry picture, and they liked each other very much and were quite inseperable ever since.

They dated and dated and moved in with each other, then they got a cat, moved again and got another cat.

Then Rallys proposed during Christmas at Svens moms house and here we are. Getting married in a tiny ass village in Germany. Therefore we created this page for you as getting there requires some detailed explanation.
